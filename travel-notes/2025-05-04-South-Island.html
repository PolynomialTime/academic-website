The Autumn of South Island, NZ

南岛的秋, 新西兰

May 4, 2025

Note 1.

We set off from Queenstown in the morning and drove to the nearby town of Arrowtown. We stopped at Lake Hayes along the way and met a friendly American guy. During our conversation, we learned that he worked in the computer industry in Boston, so we were colleagues. When we arrived in Arrowtown, we were immediately surrounded by the rich autumn colors, as if the world had been painted with yellow to deep red pigments. This picture was taken at the end of a small road in Arrowtown.

上午从皇后镇出发驱车来到周边小镇箭镇。途径海耶斯湖停车，遇到一热情美国小哥。交谈中得知其在波士顿从事计算机行业，算是遇到了同行。到达箭镇，立即被浓郁的秋色包围，世界的色彩由黄色到深红的颜料染成。此图是箭镇小路尽头所摄。

The site of Arrowtown is in the valley of Arrow River. Coming back from the small road, we walked for five minuets to a flat ground of the vally. I held up the camera, aimed at the mountain, and took the following picture.

阿罗镇的所在地位于箭河河谷之中。沿着小路返回后，我们走了五分钟到达了山谷中的平地。我举起相机，对准山峰，拍下了下面这张照片。
