CV   |   Email  |   LinkedIn   |   Google Scholar   |   Research Gate

I am currently a research fellow with the School of Computer Science, University of Auckland. I obtained my PhD in computer science from University of Auckland. I received the First Class Honours degree in computer science from University of Auckland in 2018. Before coming to New Zealand, I received the B.S. in computer science from Beijing Institute of Technology in 2017. My research interests span a spectrum of (inverse) reinforcement learning, computational game theory and multi-agent systems. Lately, my research focus is on applying reinforcement learning in large-scale multi-agent games, natural language processing and automatic reasoning.

When I am not spending time on research and teaching, I can be found as a hiker and photography lover. The collections of pictures that I took in the hike-way can be found in my personal online gallery and social media (optimised for access from China). I have hiked to many famous routes in many cities and towns across China and New Zealand.
